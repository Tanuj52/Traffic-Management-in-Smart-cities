Careers

Join our team and help us make traffic management more efficient and safer for everyone.

Traffic Controller

Job Description

As a Traffic Controller, you will be responsible for managing and directing traffic to ensure smooth and safe movement of vehicles. You will monitor traffic conditions, manage traffic signals, and coordinate with other team members to optimize traffic flow.

Responsibilities:

  • Monitor real-time traffic conditions
  • Operate traffic signal control systems
  • Coordinate with emergency response teams
  • Analyze traffic data and adjust signal timings

Other Avenues

Software Engineer

Develop and maintain our advanced traffic management software solutions. Work with cutting-edge technologies to improve traffic flow and safety.

Data Scientist

Analyze traffic data and develop models to predict traffic patterns. Help create data-driven solutions for better traffic management.

Project Manager

Lead projects that aim to improve traffic systems in urban and rural areas. Coordinate with different teams to ensure successful project delivery.
