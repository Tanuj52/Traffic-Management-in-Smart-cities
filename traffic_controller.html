Traffic Controller

Job Description

As a Traffic Controller, you will be responsible for managing and directing traffic to ensure smooth and safe movement of vehicles. You will monitor traffic conditions, manage traffic signals, and coordinate with other team members to optimize traffic flow.

Responsibilities

  • Monitor real-time traffic conditions
  • Operate traffic signal control systems
  • Coordinate with emergency response teams
  • Analyze traffic data and adjust signal timings

Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Valid traffic controller certification
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Ability to work under pressure
